– When I joined Plunk I was employee number 8, and to this day I'm still one of only two designers on the team. It's my role to intake a variety of data, and tell a meaningful and useful story with it.
– Every good story, deals with the past, the present, and the future.

chapter 01: The Past

Data has a strange relationship with the past. Historical information is key to current and future information, however the problem with historical information is that becuase it's already happened, there's a lot of it.

chapter 02: The Present

Plunk's mission is to provide "Real estate data in real-time". Needless to say, current and live data is the most important part of this story.

chapter 03: The Future

The future holds potential, but the potential is unknown. It's imperative that any data and insights stemming from the future are explained and easily understood.
