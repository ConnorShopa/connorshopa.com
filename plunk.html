Design + Style System

Every story, requires the fundamentals of language.

enter: the style system

Typography was seperated by each use case of the lettering. Body text was given room to breathe, while headings were made bold and slightly squished with the purpose of acting as seperation in the product.

Colors were labeled, and made into elements. This ensured accurate usage of the same palette spanning the entire suite of products. Gradients and tones were added for more flexibility across the use cases.

With the style and tone of the product set into a nomenclature, sketches and ideation began. Issues of creating small elements from scratch quickly became apparent, on top of a growing seperation of product fundamentals.

enter: the design system

Following the atomic design methodology allowed for a more comprehensive break down of the many parts that make up both Plunk's identity, and product language.

The fundamental parts of a design system. Atoms represent the smallest components that are added together to create something more.

Moleclues are atoms put together. Working as singluar components that produce parts of a product, but not the whole.

Organisms are the largest combination of singluar elements. They allow for parts of pages to be constructed.

Pages see organisms living together. Taking all singular elements and building interfaces and usable products.

the living being

Using Figma's component system, and including interaction flows, allowed for fast ideation that remained in the target product language.

When I joined Plunk I was employee number 8, and to this day I'm still one of only two designers on the team. It's my role to intake a variety of data, and tell a meaningful and useful story with it.

Every good story, deals with the past, the present, and the future.

chapter 01: The Past

Data has a strange relationship with the past. Historical information is key to current and future information, however the problem with historical information is that becuase it's already happened, there's a lot of it.

chapter 02: The Present

Plunk's mission is to provide "Real estate data in real-time". Needless to say, current and live data is the most important part of this story.

chapter 03: The Future

The future holds potential, but the potential is unknown. It's imperative that any data and insights stemming from the future are explained and easily understood.
