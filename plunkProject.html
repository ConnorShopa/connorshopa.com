Plunk Product Roadmap

Plunk was in the middle of a shift of both audiences and product focus. I was responsible for ideating, designing, proposing and handing off product designs and ideas.

Process

Design + Style System

To facilitate the ideation and shipping of new in-house products, a design and style system was built. It's goal was to:

1. – Allow the product team to quickly ideate and propose new products while maintaining alignment with the Plunk brand.

2. – Bridge the gap between development and design teams.

View Design System

Insights

Plunk's mission was to help homeowners understand their home as a financial asset through the use of A.I to understand, forecast and explain residential data.

I was tasked with intaking these data sets, and outputting a series of products that visually conveyed the information to homeowners.

Housing the insights

Plunk Pro™ was created to become a home for Plunk's past, present and future offerings.

It's goal was to allow audiences to understand unique product offerings, create a free account, and explore what Plunk had to offer.

Plunk Pro™ Mobile

Plunk Pro™ mobile is a companion app for Plunk Pro.

It allowed audiences to view key information on the go, receive updates and notifications about saved homes, as well as view key data at a glance through the use of homescreen widgets.
