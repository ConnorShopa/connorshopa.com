PROBLEM

In early parts of summer 2023, Spotify underwent a redesign to its desktop client. The goal of the rework was to allow users to find music + content faster. The entire experience was rethought, all except fullscreen mode.

PLAN

Design a new fullscreen experience that meshes with current Spotify product and experience. The targer of the project is to create a potential next series of designs for fullscreen experience. Success means creating a comprehesive design that improves the current expreience.

BREAKDOWN

In order to create a functional fullscreen design, It’s important to take a look at current interface. This allows for targetting areas that need improvement, while also highlighting elements that work and can contiune to work.

INTENTION

Fullscreen is a celebration of current song being played. Displaying song or content art as main focal point.
It exists as a medium to focus on the music that is being played. Purposefully lacking focus on the rest of the Spotify functionality.

POSSIBILITIES

Add social functionality, allowing for listening parties.
Continue a visually muted-art central display of content. Design an experience that users will want to interact with.

SUMMARY

Fullscreen currently exists as a visually focused screen that displays very little functionality.
My target : Create an art-central interface that allows users to build a display of their collections.
