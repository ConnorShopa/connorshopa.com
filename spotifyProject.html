Record shelf

Over the last year, I've become fascinated with vinyl-records. Both the aspect of collecting physical objects that hold the power music has, and the fun of exploring and sifting through crates when I visit record-stores.

This fascination led to the idea of bringing these same experiences to Spotify. I aimed to create a digital manifestation of the record-crate, for two purposes:

– Organizing collections in a physical way

– Add joy to the discovery of new content through thumbing through never-before seen things
